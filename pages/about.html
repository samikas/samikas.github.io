About me



I'm a Front end developer from Espoo. I'm interested in web development and design, editing videos, pictures and music.

In 2014 I was a graphic designer trainee at Nelonen Media for six months. During that time I produced graphics for television, magazines and websites. I also took part in many advertisement video shoots and learnt a lot during that time.

During my free time I like to play guitar, sing, compose and record music, play some disc golf and floorball.
